# KidWay Bounded Contexts

This frontend base includes folders for the 16 KidWay bounded contexts identified in the design-level EventStorming session:

1. Identity & Access Management
2. User Profiles
3. Subscription & Payments
4. Dashboard
5. Fleet Management
6. Driver Management
7. Route Management
8. Student Management
9. Assignment Management
10. Real-Time Tracking
11. Trip Management
12. Attendance Tracking
13. Alerts & Notifications
14. Incident Management
15. Analytics & Reports
16. Company Management

IAM and User Profiles are configured outside the sidebar because login/register belong to the authentication flow and profile settings belong to the topbar.
